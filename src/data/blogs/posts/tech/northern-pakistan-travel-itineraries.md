Northern Pakistan offers some of the world's most breathtaking scenery. Here are the best ways to structure your trip.

## Itinerary Options
| Duration | Focus | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **8 Days** | Fairy Meadows & Hunza | First-timers wanting iconic views quickly. |
| **12 Days** | Skardu + Hunza | Balanced tour of major valleys and forts. |
| **14+ Days** | Trekking & Culture | Adventurous travelers adding Swat/Kalash or base camp treks. |
| **3-4 Weeks** | The Grand Tour | Complete North + Cities (Lahore/Karachi). |
