Building an e-commerce store in Pakistan is a strategic process that involves careful planning and execution. The following flowchart outlines the key phases, from validating your idea to scaling your established business.

![E-commerce Flowchart](https://i.postimg.cc/8kLPmh4D/example.png)

## Phase 1: Validate Your Business Idea & Niche
*   **Research:** Use Google Trends and Daraz best-sellers.
*   **Test:** Run a small WhatsApp/Insta campaign before building a site.

## Phase 2: Foundation
*   **Register:** Get your NTN from FBR.
*   **Payments:** Set up COD (essential) and digital gateways (JazzCash/PayFast).

## Phase 3: Build Your Store
*   **Shopify:** Best all-in-one for beginners.
*   **WooCommerce:** Great if you want customization on WordPress.
*   **Local:** Try Webx.pk for local integrations.

## Phase 4: Operations
*   **Logistics:** Partner with TCS, Leopards, or Call Courier.
*   **Packaging:** Branded, sturdy packaging builds trust.

## Phase 5: Launch & Market
*   **Mix:** Use Insta/TikTok for visuals, Performance Marketing (Meta Ads) for sales.
*   **Marketplaces:** List on Daraz to capture their traffic, then funnel to your site.
