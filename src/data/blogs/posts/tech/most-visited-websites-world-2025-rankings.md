Beyond the top tier, several points stand out from the data:

*   **AI's Notable Entry:** The inclusion of ChatGPT.com in the global top 10 is a significant event for 2025, highlighting the massive public interest in generative AI. Other sources also note OpenAI.com as a rapidly rising site.
*   **Strong Regional Players:** Search engines like Yandex.ru (Russia) and Baidu.com (China) rank highly globally, reflecting substantial regional internet ecosystems.
*   **Dominant Categories:** The lists are overwhelmingly populated by Search Engines (Google, Bing), Social Media/Video platforms (YouTube, Facebook, Instagram), and E-commerce giants (Amazon).
*   **Why the Data Varies:** The differences between rankings are normal. They are due to factors like different data providers (Semrush vs. Similarweb), varying measurement methodologies, and the specific months the data was collected.
