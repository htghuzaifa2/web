Traveling in Pakistan is always an adventure — whether it’s exploring the mountains of Gilgit-Baltistan or the historical streets of Lahore. Packing smart makes trips more enjoyable. Here’s a checklist to help you cover the essentials.

## 1. Travel Documents & Essentials
*   ID & Passport (for Gilgit-Baltistan/borders).
*   Tickets & Itinerary (digital + print).
*   Cash (for small towns).
*   Emergency contacts.

## 2. Clothing Essentials
Depends on season/destination, but basics include:
*   Comfortable tees/pants (Jeans).
*   Light jacket/sweater (Nights get chilly up north).
*   Shalwar Kameez (for traditional towns).
*   Walking shoes + Sandals/Flip-flops.
*   **Tip:** Layering is key for mountains.

## 3. Toiletries & Personal Care
*   Toothbrush, soap, shampoo.
*   Sunscreen & lip balm.
*   Hand sanitizer & wet wipes.
*   **Pro Tip:** Use travel-sized bottles.

## 4. Tech & Gadgets
*   Phone + Power bank.
*   Earphones.
*   Travel adapter.
*   Camera (optional).

## 5. Travel & Health Essentials
*   First-aid kit (painkillers, bandages).
*   Reusable water bottle.
*   Snacks (nuts, biscuits).
*   Sunglasses & hat.

## 6. Optional Items for Convenience
*   Foldable bag for souvenirs.
*   Travel pillow.
*   Offline maps.

### A Little Reflection
Packing well is about thinking ahead for comfort and safety. A checklist makes trips smoother so you can focus on the experience.
