## üí° Detailed Tax-Saving Strategies

### For Everyone: Deductions and Credits
*   **Zakat:** Deductions are fully deductible from total income.
*   **Charitable Donations:** Donations to approved institutions qualify for a tax credit.
*   **Voluntary Pension Scheme (VPS):** Contributions can earn a tax credit of up to 20% of taxable income.
*   **Life & Health Insurance:** Premiums for approved policies are also deductible.

### For Freelancers: The PSEB Advantage
If you earn in foreign currency, registering with the Pakistan Software Export Board (PSEB) is crucial. It reduces your final tax rate on export earnings from 1% to just 0.25%.

### For Property Transactions: Timing and Status
*   **On Purchase:** Advance Tax for a filer is significantly lower than for a non-filer.
*   **On Sale:** Capital Gains Tax decreases the longer you hold the property.
