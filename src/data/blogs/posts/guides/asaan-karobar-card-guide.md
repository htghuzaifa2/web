## ‚úÖ Who Can Get This Card?
The program specifically aims to help those who often find it hard to get loans from traditional banks. You're likely eligible if you:
*   Are a resident of Punjab with a valid CNIC.
*   Are between 25 and 55 years old.
*   Run a small shop, are a freelancer, a home-based worker (like a tailor or artisan), or work in the gig economy (like a delivery rider).
*   Have a feasible business idea or an existing business.
*   Belonging to a priority group like women, youth, or differently-abled persons is a plus.
