I still upload posts from a 3-G dongle in Lahore’s hostel; if the steps below work there, they’ll work on your fiber too.
Follow A → Z, tick each box, hit Publish.

## 1. Pre-upload prep (5-minute ritual)
*   **Images:** Compress to WebP < 100 KB using **tool.huzi.pk**.
*   **Filename:** `urdu-keyword-english.jpg` (e.g., `best-biryani-karachi-2025.webp`).
*   **Thumbnails:** 1200 × 628 px.

## 2. WordPress dashboard walk-through
*   **Title:** Keyword first + curiosity hook.
*   **Permalink:** Clean slug (remove stop-words).
*   **Category:** One only.
*   **Tags:** < 8 relevant tags.

## 3. Content structure (copy-paste ready)
*   **Intro:** Keyword in first 40-60 words.
*   **H2/H3:** Use headers for hierarchy.
*   **Table:** Price comparisons.
*   **FAQ:** 5 questions with schema.
*   **Internal Link:** 1 old post.
*   **External Link:** 1 authority (nofollow).

## 4. On-page SEO plugin settings (Yoast / RankMath)
*   **Meta Description:** Include keyword, < 155 chars.
*   **Focus Keyword:** Set it and check for green lights.

## 5. Image optimization inside WP
*   **Alt text:** Descriptive + keyword.
*   **Lazy-load:** Enabled via LiteSpeed Cache.

## 6. Schema & social
*   **RankMath:** Article schema.
*   **FAQ Block:** Generates FAQPage schema for voice search.

## 7. URL upload & Google ping
*   **Index:** Request indexing in Search Console immediately after publishing.

## 8. Post-upload promo
*   Share on FB, Twitter, LinkedIn, and WhatsApp groups with a short link.

## 9. Performance check (Day 7)
*   Check Impressions and CTR in Search Console.
*   Optimize if necessary.

## 10. Maintenance
*   Update year in title annually.
*   Check for broken links bi-annually.

Upload today, tick every box, and watch your traffic grow!
