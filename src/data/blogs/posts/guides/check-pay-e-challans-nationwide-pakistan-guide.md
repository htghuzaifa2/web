While the specific portals vary, the process for checking and paying e-challans is generally similar across Pakistan. You can expect to follow these steps:

1.  **Find the Official Portal:** Visit the website of the relevant traffic police authority (e.g., Punjab Safe Cities Authority for Lahore, Sindh Police for Karachi).
2.  **Check Your Challan:** Look for a section like "E-Challan," "Traffic Fine," or "Challan Status." You can typically search using your vehicle registration number, CNIC, or a specific challan number.
3.  **View Details and Pay:** The system will show any pending fines with details (date, location, violation). You can then pay online using a credit/debit card, mobile wallet, or bank transfer.
4.  **Save the Receipt:** Always download or save the payment confirmation receipt as proof.
