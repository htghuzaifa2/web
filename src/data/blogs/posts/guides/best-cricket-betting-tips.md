Here is a breakdown of educational tips and essential ethical practices.

Understanding the risks and ethical implications is non-negotiable for any fan.

### For You, The Bettor:

*   **Bet for Entertainment, Not Income:** Treat betting as a paid form of engagement, not a revenue stream. Chasing losses can quickly lead to significant financial and emotional stress.
*   **Use Responsible Gambling Tools:** Reputable platforms offer self-exclusion options, deposit limits, and time-outs. Using these is a sign of smart control.
*   **Recognize Problem Gambling:** Warning signs include spending more than intended, lying about betting, and borrowing money to bet. If you recognize these, seek help immediately.

### For the Sport's Integrity:

*   **Avoid Illegal Operators:** Bet only with licensed, regulated bookmakers. Illegal betting is often linked to match-fixing and organized crime, which destroys the sport's credibility.
*   **Uphold Fair Play:** The ICC's Code of Ethics emphasizes honesty and integrity as the sport's foundation. As a fan, you support this by betting legally and rejecting any insider information.
